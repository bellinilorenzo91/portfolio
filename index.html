Lorenzo Bellini

I work as a graphic designer at IKEA San Giuliano, where I design and install physical and digital communications following the brand guidelines, navigating the fast-paced retail environment and customer experience dynamics. I occasionally collaborate with an art high school, conducting workshops and lectures for the graphic design department. Visual design represents a point of arrival for me: my background is deeply rooted in art, from academy painting to curatorial practice, an area that still fuels and influences my graphic design work.

  • 01. IKEA CCO Project:

    A case study and pilot project selected to synthesize my daily role as an in-house Graphic Designer for the IKEA store in San Giuliano Milanese, highlighting the management of complex, large-scale visual solutions.

    Year: 2025 — Type: Retail Graphic Design / Case Study

  • 02. Xin, Il cuore come sede della mente:

    An independent editorial project and experimental zine driven by personal research. Through a poetic exploration that brings together materials collected during a journey — photographs, collages, and text fragments — the layout is structured to create an immersive experience based on free associations and visual evocations.

    Year: 2025/2026 — Type: Editorial Design / Zine

  • 03. Graphic Design Workshop:

    Video documentation of a graphic design workshop. This content is part of an ongoing three-year collaboration built on periodic teaching interventions and laboratory consultations with Liceo Artistico Preziosissimo Sangue in Monza.

    Year: 2026 — Type: Education / Video Documentation
